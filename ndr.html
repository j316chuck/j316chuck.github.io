Cyber Watchman

Product

The Cyber watchman product aims to automate network detection reponse systems with large language models. Our proposed solution involves utilizing large language models (LLMs) trained on abstract network metadata to detect cyber attacks and automate the response, thereby significantly improving efficiency and reducing manual interventions. Given the over 173 billion dollar cyber security market, we believe that this largely untapped market will be disrupted by the recent advent of more powerful language agents.
